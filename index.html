Our Mission

The objective of LBG is to inculcate bits of professionalism in Engineering students by teaching them deep English concepts.

Our Goal

The goal is to cause a change in the lives of the students, which will be beneficial for their profession. Thus, helping them to become globally aware and infusing sensitivity towards mankind.

Our Focus

We desire to develop problem-solving and strategic thinking in their young minds. LBG will also help them in logic-building and decision-making, which is really important in day-to-day life.

Our Aim

Our objective is to instil a good vocabulary into the student by using games in an interactive peer-to-peer environment.
