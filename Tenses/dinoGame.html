rules of the game
  • To start game click on the start button. The stones will start moving.
  • You have to collide with the stone with correct answer.
  • Jump over stones with the wrong answers.(stones with wrong answers are obstacles)
  • Click on the next button for attempting next question.
    • ↑
    press UP-ArrowKey to jump dino up.
    ←
    press LEFT-ArrowKey to move dino left.
    →
    press RIGHT-ArrowKey to move dino right.
