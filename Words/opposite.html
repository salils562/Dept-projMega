How to play ?
  • There are total 4-words in 4-sided boxes
  • Among 4-words , two words are synonyms and two are antonyms to the word at center
  • You have to select any one Mode : Synonym mode or Antonym mode
  • Synonym Mode: The words that are similar to center word will be correct.
  • Antonym Mode: The words that are opposite to center word will be correct.
